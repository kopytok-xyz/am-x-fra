FAQ

Find quick answers to common questions about Alinom

What is the difference between Alinom and open-source solutions?

While Alinom licenses are free, like open-source software, we do not provide access to the source code of our core platform to ensure product quality and security.

Where can I get the latest software news and updates?

Stay up to date with the latest Alinom news and updates by following us on our social media channels and YouTube.

What support packages does Alinom offer?

Alinom offers three tiers of L3 support packages: Gold, Silver, and Bronze. Contact us via the form on our website to discuss support package options and pricing.

How can I integrate Alinom with my existing OSS solutions?

Alinom features out-of-the-box integrations with external systems (Service Desks, NRIs, CMDBs, etc.) and provides an open integration platform for custom implementations.

What professional services are available for purchase?

Our professional team offers L3 support services, implementation assistance, customization, and integration services. New service requests can be submitted through our certified partners.

What technologies does Alinom cover by default?

Alinom provides default coverage for a wide range of technologies, including IP/MPLS networks, servers, virtual infrastructures, storage systems, SAN networks, transport networks, VoIP, wireless networks, various applications, and end-user experience testing.

How can I become certified?

Alinom offers three levels of certification: Operator, Administrator, and Developer. Request details about training programs and certification options via the contact form.

How do I become a partner?

To become an Alinom partner, please complete the partnership request form on our website. We will contact you shortly to discuss collaboration opportunities.

How can I access product manuals?

Product manuals are available through our authorized partners. Please request access via the contact form on our website.

Are there any functional or licensing limitations?

No, Alinom software has no functional limitations. All features are available out of the box with the free license.

How can I download the software?

Alinom software is available for free download through our authorized partners. Simply fill out the request form on our website to get started.
